About

Prof.Dr.S.A.Veerapandian alias Dr Vee was born and brought up at Tamilnadu, India. Evolving as Music Information Technologist With more than 30 years of teaching undergraduate courses & 25 years of teaching post graduate courses in Physics, Dr.Vee had handled all the subjects in Physics, including Digital Electronics & Applied Electronics (both theory & practical), enabling the development as the Music Information Technologist, after the completion of the Ph.D involving Physics & Music; apart from serving as the member of the Board of Studies in Physics & Music, in the University of Madras.

Research Interest:

  • Music
  • Linguistics
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Ancient texts of Tamil & Sanskrit Indology
  • Indology
  • Socio-Physics
