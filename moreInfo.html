Dr.Vee founded the first south indian music website ‘www.southindianmusic.com’ in 1998 and online music journal ‘Music Mail’; Now continuing as “www.southindianmusic.org” (Website data migration on progress) Dr Vee debut Instrumental Music album ‘Let’s Go Green’ released at 1999 and its live performance held at Chennai Museum Theatre, India. Dr.Vee had composed Music for International Award winning video documentary ‘Stagnation’ and Historical documentary ‘Poompuhar’. Dr.Vee had conducted several workshops, published articles in many journals, national and inter-national conferences, and books in English & Tamil. Currently Dr Vee is providing consultancy services for Music Related R & D projects, Music Composing and Music related workshops.
